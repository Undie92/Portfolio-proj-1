About me



Hello and welcome to my homepage, i am Rebecka Hellström, born in 1993. I'm a proud mother of a 2 year old girl, working at a full time job and make art on my spare free time.


I started painting already when i was a small girl in school, and always had a big interest in exploring new ways to learn and master the pencil.


As i grew, i became better and better at producing finished art projects and developing my skills even further. And as you can see in the gallery page, i'd say i'm quite decent at painting now, still learning new stuff every time i draw.
